Group by tier. Only pursue tier 1 & 2. Track in [[backlink-outreach]].

- Junebug Weddings — junebugweddings.com
- Green Wedding Shoes — greenweddingshoes.com
- Style Me Pretty — stylemepretty.com
- Rock My Wedding — rockmywedding.co.uk
- Wedding Chicks — weddingchicks.com
- Ruffled — ruffledblog.com

## Tier 2 — planner / vendor blogs (DR 40+)

- Aisle Planner blog
- HoneyBook journal
- The Planner's Vault
- WeddingPro (The Knot / WeddingWire vendor blog)

## Tier 3 — HARO / Qwoted quotes

- Journalists writing on: wedding tech, guest experience, memory keeping,
  post-pandemic weddings, elopements, multi-cultural weddings

## Pitch angles

- "Voice-first guest book: why couples are moving away from paper" — trend piece
- "How to modernize the guest book without losing sentiment" — practical guide
- "Photography-friendly guest engagement" — photographer angle
- "Multi-lingual weddings: capturing every voice" — international angle
- Ovation founder story — origin/vision piece

## Do NOT pursue

- Fiverr link packages
- PBNs
- Reciprocal link exchanges (unless natural fit)
- Comment/forum spam
- Low-DR wedding directory listings that require payment
